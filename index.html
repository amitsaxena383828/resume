 DON'T TOUCH
 (Security App)

It is an Android application called Don't Touch. It’s a security utility designed to protect your device from theft. The app leverages two key features: charger unplug detection and motion sensing. When activated, Don't Touch triggers an emergency alarm if the device is unplugged while charging. Similarly, if the phone is placed on a table or surface and someone tries to touch it, the app detects the motion and sounds an alarm at full volume, alerting you to any potential tampering.
